INDIAN PENAL CODE

Section 420 Cheating and dishonestly inducing delivery of property
Whoever cheats and dishonestly induces a person to deliver property shall be punished with imprisonment up to seven years and fine.

Section 406 Criminal breach of trust
Dishonest misappropriation of entrusted property is punishable with imprisonment or fine.

Section 409 Criminal breach of trust by public servant
Public servants committing breach of trust face severe punishment including life imprisonment.

Section 378 Theft
Taking movable property without consent with dishonest intention is theft.

Section 379 Punishment for theft
Punishable with imprisonment up to three years or fine.

Section 380 Theft in dwelling house
Theft inside a house or building is punishable with higher penalties.

Section 392 Robbery
Theft accompanied by violence or threat is robbery.

Section 394 Voluntarily causing hurt in robbery
Causing injury during robbery increases punishment.

Section 415 Cheating
Deceiving a person to deliver property or consent is cheating.

Section 463 Forgery
Creating false documents with intent to cause harm is forgery.

Section 465 Punishment for forgery
Forgery is punishable with imprisonment or fine.

Section 468 Forgery for purpose of cheating
Forgery intended for cheating is punishable with imprisonment up to seven years.

Section 471 Using forged document
Using forged documents as genuine is punishable.

Section 499 Defamation
Harming someone's reputation through statements is defamation.

Section 500 Punishment for defamation
Punishable with imprisonment up to two years or fine.

Section 503 Criminal intimidation
Threatening a person with injury is criminal intimidation.

Section 506 Punishment for criminal intimidation
Punishment depends on severity of threat.

Section 299 Culpable homicide
Causing death with intention or knowledge constitutes culpable homicide.

Section 300 Murder
Culpable homicide becomes murder under specific conditions.

Section 302 Punishment for murder
Punishable with death or life imprisonment.

Section 304 Culpable homicide not amounting to murder
Punishment varies based on intention and knowledge.

Section 323 Voluntarily causing hurt
Causing physical pain or injury is punishable.

Section 325 Grievous hurt
Serious injuries attract stricter punishment.

Section 341 Wrongful restraint
Restricting someone’s movement unlawfully is punishable.

Section 351 Assault
Threatening or attempting to cause harm is assault.

Section 354 Assault on woman
Outraging modesty of a woman is punishable.

Section 354A Sexual harassment
Unwelcome advances or physical contact constitute sexual harassment.

Section 354D Stalking
Repeatedly following or contacting a woman is punishable.

Section 376 Rape
Punishable with rigorous imprisonment not less than ten years.

Section 509 Insulting modesty of woman
Words or gestures insulting modesty are punishable.

Section 34 Common intention
All persons involved in a crime share liability.
